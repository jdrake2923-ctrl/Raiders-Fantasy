⚽ PLAYER POINTS (per match)
Goal scored+1
Official goals only. Own goals do not count for the scorer.
Source: Official FIFA match report
Assist+1
The final pass directly leading to a goal. Must be awarded in official match data. Second assists do not count.
Source: API-Football match statistics
Win or Draw (played)+1
Player must appear in the match. Awarded if team wins or draws.
Source: Official match lineup
Played 60+ minutes+1
Player must be on the pitch for at least 60 minutes including stoppage time.
Source: Official substitution records
Every 20 passes completed+1
Total successful passes (short, medium, long) per 20. Example: 40 passes = +2, 60 passes = +3.
Source: API-Football passes.total
Pass accuracy ≥ 80%+1
Percentage of attempted passes reaching a teammate. Minimum 80% required.
Source: API-Football passes.accuracy
2+ successful dribbles+1
Player beats an opponent 1v1 while maintaining possession. Minimum 2 completed dribbles required.
Source: API-Football dribbles.success / Sofascore
3+ tackles / challenges won+1
Player cleanly takes the ball from an opponent. Minimum 3 won tackles required.
Source: API-Football tackles.total
2+ interceptions+1
Player cuts out a pass or cross intended for an opponent. Minimum 2 required.
Source: API-Football tackles.interceptions
3+ aerial duels won+1
Player wins a header challenge against an opponent in the air. Minimum 3 required.
Source: API-Football duels.won
2+ key passes+1
Pass that directly creates a goal-scoring opportunity (shot). Does not need to result in a goal. Minimum 2 required.
Source: API-Football passes.key
Shot on target — FWD (non-goal)+0.5
Forwards only. Shot that would have gone in if not saved. Goals do not earn this bonus separately.
Source: API-Football shots.on
Man of the Match+2
Awarded by FIFA/UEFA to the best player each match. One award per game only.
Source: Official FIFA match report
Clean sheet — Outfield player+1
Team does not concede. Player must play the full 90 minutes to qualify.
Source: Official match result
Clean sheet — Goalkeeper+3
Goalkeeper's team does not concede in 90 minutes. Must play the full match.
Source: Official match result
Every 3 saves — Goalkeeper+1
GK prevents a goal-bound shot from entering the net. Per every 3 saves. Example: 3 saves = +1, 6 saves = +2.
Source: API-Football goals.saves
Penalty saved — Goalkeeper+3
GK saves a penalty in normal play or extra time. Shootout penalties do not count.
Source: Official match events
Yellow card−1
Official caution issued by the referee.
Source: Official match events
Red card−2
Player is sent off. Includes straight red cards and second yellow cards.
Source: Official match events
📊 DATA & DISPUTE RESOLUTION
All statistics are sourced from API-Football (api-sports.io), which aggregates official FIFA and UEFA match data. Scores update automatically within minutes of each match ending.

To verify any stat independently, check Sofascore.com or FBref.com using the same match.

In the event of a dispute, the official FIFA match report is the final authority. Commissioner decisions on scoring disputes are final.
